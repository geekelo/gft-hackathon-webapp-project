Global Female Tech Hackathon

The Global Female Tech Hackathon provides a platform for participants to showcase their innovative projects to a panel of esteemed judges. The winning teams receive recognition, mentorship opportunities, and support to further develop their solutions and bring them to market.

2023.07.03(Mon) - 7(Fri)

@Kingsway Palace, Lekki, Lagos State. Nigeria

Hackathon Stages

Ideation and Team Formation

Attendees share their skills and interests, and teams are formed based on complementary skill sets.

Project Development

Participants work collaboratively to build their projects, utilizing their technical skills and creativity.

Mentoring and Workshops

Mentors help refine project ideas, offer technical advice, and provide insights based on their industry experience.

Testing and Iteration

Teams seek feedback from mentors, other participants, and even potential users to identify areas for improvement and refine their prototypes.

Presentation and Judging

Judges evaluate the projects based on criteria such as innovation, technical implementation, user experience, and potential for scalability.
