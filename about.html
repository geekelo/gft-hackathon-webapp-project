About The Global Female Tech Hackathon

The Global Female Tech Hackathon is an inclusive and groundbreaking event designed to empower women in the field of technology. With the goal of narrowing the gender gap and promoting diversity within the tech industry, this hackathon brings together brilliant minds from around the world to collaborate, innovate, and make a lasting impact.

Please contact us via Email for further questions

info@gfthackathon.org

Features of The Global Females Tech Hackathon

The event features workshops and mentorship sessions by industry experts, equipping participants with the necessary knowledge and guidance to develop their ideas and transform them into viable solutions.

Purpose for The Global Females Tech Hackathon

Ultimately, the Global Female Tech Hackathon serves as a catalyst for change, encouraging women to take a leading role in shaping the future of technology.

Vision

The winning teams receive recognition, mentorship opportunities, and support to further develop their solutions and bring them to market.

Mission

To bridge the gender gap, promote diversity, and inspire the next generation of female leaders in tech
